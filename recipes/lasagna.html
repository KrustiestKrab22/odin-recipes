Yummy Lasagna

Description

The photo above depicts a longstanding Italian delicacy, the humble lasagna. Isn't it delicious?

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta
  • Ground Beef
  • Marinara
  • Ricotta
  • Mozzarella

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400degF
  2. Boil pasta until al dente
  3. Cook ground beef until brown; drain
  4. Simmer marinara with ground beef added
  5. Alternate layering pasta, ricotta, and sauce
  6. Top with Mozzarella
  7. Bake for 45 minutes
  8. Enjoy!
