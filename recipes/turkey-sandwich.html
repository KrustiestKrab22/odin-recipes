Turkey Sandwich

Description

The photo above depicts a ubiquitous lunch option, the turkey sandwich. Isn't it delicious?

Ingredients

  • Sliced sandwich bread
  • Deli Turkey; sliced or chopped
  • Deli sliced cheese; your preference
  • Mayonnaise
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato

Steps

  1. Lay out sandwich bread on cooking surface
  2. Spread a thin layer of mayonnaise on both slices of bread
  3. Lay preferred amount of cheese directly on top of mayonnaise
  4. Add desired amount of deli turkey
  5. Place tomatoes directly on deli turkey
  6. Finish with thin bed of lettuce
  7. Cover sandwich with other slice of bread
  8. Optional: Slice into triangles or rectangles
  9. Enjoy!
